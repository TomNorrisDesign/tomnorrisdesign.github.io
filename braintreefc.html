Badge Redesign | Braintree Town FC

This is my first badge redesign project, and I managed to complete it in just a few days. It was a great learning curve for me, and I can already see areas where I could do things differently and improve next time. Working on this has shown me the importance of balancing creativity with practicality, especially when redesigning something as meaningful as a football club badge.

For Braintree Town FC, my goal was to modernise the badge while still keeping the key elements that make it recognisable and unique to the club. One of the main changes was updating the colours to better match the club's current branding, which helps tie everything together visually. I also decided to use a more modern typeface, Creato Display Bold, which gives the badge a contemporary feel.

Throughout the process, I focused on creating a design that would work well across different applications, whether that’s on a kit, merchandise, or digital platforms. It was important to make sure the badge would look sharp and professional in any context, while still reflecting the club’s heritage. I’ve learnt a lot about how small design choices can make a big impact, and this project has given me valuable insight into how to approach similar tasks in the future.

Overall, I’m pleased with the result and proud to have taken on this challenge. It’s exciting to see the skills I have learnt through this project, and I'm looking forward to see what I can improve on in my next one.
