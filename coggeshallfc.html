Badge Redesign | Coggeshall Town FC

This was a personal project where I gave the Coggeshall Town FC badge a slight redesign. The original badge already had a strong identity, but I wanted to make it feel cleaner and more aligned while keeping its core look intact. It was a great way to practice working with an existing design and making subtle improvements.

One of the key changes was resizing some parts of the badge to improve the overall balance. Adjusting the proportions helped make the design feel more centred and visually consistent. I also updated the typography by using Creato Display, a font I love working with that adds a clean and modern feel.

Another important tweak was aligning the "Founded" date with other elements of the badge. This small change made the design look tidier and better put together. These types of tweaks might seem minor, but they can have a big impact on the final result.
